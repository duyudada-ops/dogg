Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Pupulation collects information you provide when creating your dog's profile, including:

  • Dog photos and descriptions
  • Basic information about your dog (breed, age, size, etc.)
  • Your contact information
  • Location data (with your permission)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and maintain your dog's profile
  • Match your dog with compatible playmates
  • Facilitate connections between dog owners
  • Improve our service

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information necessary for the app's functionality, such as showing your dog's profile to potential matches.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Control privacy settings

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@pupulation.app
